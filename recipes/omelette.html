Omelette

Description

An omelette is like a folded pancake made up of beaten eggs, sometimes accompanied with vegetables and cheese.

It is considered a healthy and delicious breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt as per required
  • Black Pepper
  • 1 chopped Onion
  • Coriander
  • Butter/ Oil

Steps

  1. Break the eggs and put them into a bowl
  2. Add salt, pepper, chopped onions and coriander to the bowl
  3. Mix it well
  4. Heat a pan, anda add one tsp of butter/oil to it
  5. Add the egg batter to the pan and let it cook on medium hit for about 3 minutes
  6. Voila!
