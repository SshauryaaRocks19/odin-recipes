Cheese Toast

Description

This grilled Cajun corn is fresh sweet corn, charred on the grill, and tossed in Cajun seasoned butterâ€”corn at its finest.

It's an easy side dish for a summer cookout. I like to dip mine in Ranch salad dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 ears corn, shucked and cleaned
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon parsley flakes, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat an outdoor grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut each ear of corn into thirds.
  3. Place corn directly on the grates of the preheated grill. Cook 15 minutes, turning every 5 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile melt butter and Old Bay in a small skillet over low heat.
  5. Transfer grilled corn to a large bowl. Drizzle seasoned butter over the top and toss to coat. Transfer to serving plate and garnish with parsley flakes.
  6. Voila!
