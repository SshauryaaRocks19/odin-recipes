Cheese Toast

Description

A cheese toast is simply a toast made up of cheese.

It is a scrumptious snack.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces shredded sharp white Cheddar heese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 slices Italian bread
  • Â¼ cup thinly sliced basil leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with foil or a silicone liner. Drizzle with 2 tablespoons olive oil.
  2. Place bread slices on the prepared baking sheet. Top each slice with sliced basil, and then Cheddar cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is well browned and crispy around the edges, 15 to 20 minutes. Let cool to room temperature.
  4. Voila!
